Compressional Bioinformatics

Last summer I worked at the San Diego Supercomputer Center on a Bioinformatics research Project, that has now received a funding of
$1 million. My role included:

  • Developing algorithms for high performance comparison and clustering of biomolecular structures.
  • Assessing biomolecule specific compression schemes and data formats.
  • Developing and benchmark approaches for hosting compressed structures.
  • Developing open source library using existing BioJava framework.

Hackspace

Hackspace is an iOS application built entirely using the Ionic Framework. The application allows attendees to browse through list of other hackers to collaborate on projects. A Facebook Login system is integrated into the application that makes it easier to access user information. Hackers can send requests and pitch ideas to other hackers to collaborate on in hackathons.
